The Defitech foundation's mission is to use computering technology to serve disabled childrens and young adults.

Defitech participates to the Flower-for-all breath project in order to build an iAPP for iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch.

This software is still in developpement, if you want to participate or comment, please contact us at: info@defitech.ch.


Notes:

Flower-for-all breath software is the result of an initiative of Brigitte Bouzin, Vincent Chritin, Eric Van Lancker, Romain Labbé and Phillip Trotter.(www.flower-for-all.org)

The Defitech foundation is not affiliated and does not have any contractual relation with the Flutter® manufacturer.
